Benedict EduCyber

A deterministic agent designed to provide personalized learning experiences. Main purpose is to support Benedict College students through guided, Socratic learning and interactive feedback.

How It Works

1. Sign in securely using your Google account.

2. Launch the AI tutor and begin your session.

3. The chatbot guides you using Socratic questioning.

4. Your interaction helps improve learning and research.

5. After your session, you’ll be asked to provide feedback.
